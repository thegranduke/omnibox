Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20/02/2025

Welcome to Omnibox. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use Omnibox.

1. Information We Collect

  • User-Provided Data: When you register, we collect your name, email, and any other details you provide.
  • Messages from Instagram: We retrieve and store messages from your Instagram profile to display them within Omnibox.
  • Analytics Data: We collect usage data to improve our service, but we do not retrieve analytics from Instagram.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide access to your social media messages within Omnibox.
  • To categorize and filter messages using AI services (Gemini AI) to enhance organization.
  • To improve our platform through internal analytics.
  • To communicate with you about service updates, security, and support.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored on a PostgreSQL database hosted on DigitalOcean.
  • All stored data is encrypted to protect your privacy.
  • Data is retained as long as you have an active account or until you request deletion.

4. AI Processing & Data Sharing

  • We share limited data with Gemini AI for message categorization.
  • AI categorizations are stored for filtering and searching purposes but can be deleted by the user.
  • We do not permanently store AI-generated insights unless necessary for service functionality.
  • AI metadata is encrypted, and we avoid storing sensitive details beyond what is required.

5. Data Deletion & User Control

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete your account and all associated data using the automated deletion button in Omnibox.
  • Specific data, including AI categorizations, can also be deleted via settings.
  • Instructions on data deletion are available in the FAQ section of our website.

To comply with GDPR and CCPA, you can request:

  • Access to your data.
  • Correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion of your personal data.

For additional requests, contact us at ltmsim001@myuct.ac.za.

6. Authentication & Security Measures

  • Omnibox stores encrypted login tokens to enable API access.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated via email or in-app notifications.

For questions or concerns, please contact us at ltmsim001@myuct.ac.za.
